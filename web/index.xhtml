Umigon - sentiment analyzer for Tweets

Umigon

Sentiment analysis for tweets, and more



- Search for a term or a person on Twitter

- Then click "Classify"!







- Want to test Umigon with your own text? Copy-paste tweets in the text input zone below.

- Then click "Classify"!
